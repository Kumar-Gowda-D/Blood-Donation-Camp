Eligibility to Donate

  • Above 55 kg of weight
  • No medicines in the last 10 days
  • Currently not having cold, cough or fever
  • No tattoos in the last 12 months
  • No alcohol or cigarette in the last 12 hours
  • Minimum 3 months gap since last donation
  • 1 week away from menstrual cycle (for girls)
